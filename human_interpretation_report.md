3. **Missense-conditioned genome scans**: Sliding-window Fst restricted to missense variants identifies adaptive protein divergence hotspots.
4. **Cross-statistic gene lookups**: After iHS/XP-EHH computation, immediate graph traversal to find associated genes.
5. **Full 1000G at scale**: 88M variants, 3,202 samples, 26 populations, 12 procedures, all in a single graph database.
